### Types of AI Avatars

- Photo Avatars: 
> Created from a static image (like a professional headshot) to which the AI adds realistic lip-syncing, blinking, and head movements. **From my point i think its the best match, since AI will only change some emotions, no video processing.**


- Video Avatars: 
>Generated using actual video footage of a real person, allowing the AI to learn their specific facial expressions, body language, and voice. 

- Stylized/Animated Avatars: 
>2D or 3D cartoon-like characters used for gaming, VTubing, or informal social media content. **I think we skip this idea since we are doing goverment app**

I will suggest Photo Avatars, since it has 
- Lowest cost
- Fastest implementation
- Consistent appearance
- Lower computational requirements
- Easier approval from legal/compliance teams
- Less risk of uncanny or inappropriate body movements
